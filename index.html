ABOUT

I am a Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) student at Carnegie Mellon University. I am a member of The CAOS Group led by Prof. Dimitrios Skarlatos.

My interests lie in computer systems and architecture. Projects I have worked on include memory defragmentation in data centers and extending the features of the hypervisor of a confidential virtual machine. I enjoy improving open source projects for system software and architectural simulation. My contribution to QEMU, OpenSBI, and the Linux kernel are in the process of being upstreamed.

In summer 2023, I worked at Rivos Inc. as a member of technical staff (software) intern in Mountain View, California.

I have two undergraduate alma maters, Columbia University and City University of Hong Kong, made possible by the joint degree program. One of the best memories of my college life was TA'ing COMS W4118: Operating Systems I twice with an amazing team in the senior year.
